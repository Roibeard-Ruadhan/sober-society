Becoming More Widespread

It has always played a huge role in many cultures across the world, specifically on social occasions. As per the video below, non alcoholic drinks are becoming more accepeted & even more appealing every day.

Walking into supermarkets now you can't help but realise, almost every week a new drink is added. Every month a new shelf is added to the non-alcohol drinks section. It is gradually taking over the whole supermarket one day at a time.
